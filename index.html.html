✦ BEING ADULT ✦

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

OVERVIEW

Being Adult is a personal finance app for tracking recurring bills and due dates. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data the app uses, where it stays, and what permissions it requires and why.

Short version: all your data lives on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

DATA STORAGE

Everything you enter — bill names, amounts, due dates, payment history — is stored in a local database on your device only. No account is required. No data is uploaded to any server.

If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

PERMISSIONS

The app requests the following permissions:

  • NOTIFICATIONS To send you bill due-date reminders and water meter reading reminders.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To deliver notifications at the precise time you set.
  • CALENDAR To optionally add bill due dates to your device calendar. Only used when you enable calendar sync. No calendar data is read beyond confirming the write succeeded.
  • CAMERA To let you photograph a bill so the app can read the amount and date via on-device text recognition. Photos are processed locally and never stored or transmitted.
  • STORAGE To let you pick an existing photo from your gallery for the same OCR feature.
  • BIOMETRIC To lock the app with your fingerprint or face if you enable the biometric lock setting. Biometric data never leaves your device — authentication is handled entirely by Android.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To reschedule notifications after the device restarts so you don't miss reminders.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app uses the following third-party components:

  • Google ML Kit (Text Recognition) — runs entirely on-device. No images or text are sent to Google servers.
  • Google Fonts — the app downloads the Press Start 2P font from Google Fonts on first launch. After that it is cached locally. See Google's Privacy Policy.

No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Being Adult is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
